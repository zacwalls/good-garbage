WHAT WE DO

OUR SERVICES

We can haul away almost anything! Items such as miscelanieous household objects, furniture, construction and yard debris, and much, much more! The list of items we cannot take is much shorter. Items such as:

Primer, first coat, flat paint, eggshell, gloss, and spraypaint

AA, AAA, D, and so on (car batteries are ok!)

T12, T8, T5 tube lights as well as blacklights (LEDs and other lighting systems accepted)

Hand sanatizer, soaps, bleach, bathroom cleaners and other household chemicals

Some items may require testing before we can take them such as old wooden window glassing, vynil and linoleum, laugh and plaster, popcorn texture on drywall, and roof tar paper
